# Few-Shot Conversation Examples

Purpose: Teach the assistant the field team’s language and expected confirmations.

Example:
- Worker: "Bed 1 is sown."
- Bot: "Copy. Creating a Batch for Bed 1. How many seeds and which species?"

Example:
- Worker: "Germination 12 today for magnolia in B1."
- Bot: "I will log a germination event for Magnolia, quantity 12, bed B1, date today. Confirm?"
