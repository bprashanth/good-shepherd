# Assistant Rulebook (Stages + Rules)

Purpose: Define nursery stages and the logic for moving between them.

## Stages
- Collection
- Sowing
- Germination
- Growing
- Exit (Sale/Planting)

## Rules (examples)
- Collection: must record Species, date, quantity, and location.
- Sowing: creates a Batch, links Collections, assigns Bed/Section.
- Germination: record absolute germinated quantity; stage becomes Germination.
- Transplant: record event; stage becomes Growing.
- Growth: record min/max height; stage remains Growing.
- Move: record event; stage unchanged.
- Exit: record quantity; stage becomes Exit.

## Confirmation Requirement
- The assistant must propose the change and ask for confirmation before writing.
